Privacy Policy for RunOrb App

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RunOrb ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services. By using RunOrb, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Phone number, email address, username, and nickname when you register.
  • Profile Information: Gender, age group, country, and city (optional).
  • Performance Data: Energy Index scores, rotation speed (RPM), and distance data recorded during RunOrb exercise sessions via Bluetooth-connected devices.
  • Media Content: Videos and images you voluntarily upload to share on the platform.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Bluetooth Data: Connection data from RunOrb gyroball devices for exercise tracking.
  • Usage Data: App interaction patterns, feature usage, and crash reports.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and local storage to maintain your session and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the RunOrb app and its features
  • Calculate and display Energy Index scores on leaderboards
  • Enable real-time PK (competition) matches between users
  • Process and display your exercise performance data
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, and new features
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Public Leaderboards: Your nickname, city, country, and Energy Index scores are displayed publicly on leaderboards as a core feature of the app.
  • PK Matches: Your performance data is shared with matched opponents during real-time competitions.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics), subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Aggregated Data: We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you individually for analytical purposes.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • We retain your account data for as long as your account is active.
  • Exercise performance data is retained indefinitely to maintain historical leaderboard records.
  • You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time by contacting us at support@runorb.us.
  • Upon account deletion request, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
  • Leaderboard entries associated with deleted accounts may be anonymized rather than removed to preserve ranking integrity.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication with token-based session management
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing where consent is the legal basis.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@runorb.us. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

RunOrb is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. Users aged 13-17 should have parental consent before using the app.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including compliance with applicable data protection laws such as GDPR and local regulations.

9. Third-Party Links

The RunOrb app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification or email for material changes

Your continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
